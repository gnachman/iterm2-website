Version 3.5.15 of iTerm2 was built on August 20, 2025

- Updated support for gpt-5 and support GPT
  hosted on github.
- Fix a bug where buttons in a selected command
  did not appear.
- When copying a modified profile to a shared
  profile, do not copy over the "Jump to this
  session" hotkey.
- Improved rendering of certain powerline glyphs.
